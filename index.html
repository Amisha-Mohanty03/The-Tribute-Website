So, Let’s get started. APJ Abdul Kalam was born on 15 October, 1931 in a middle-class Tamil family in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He completed his graduation in Physics from St. Joseph’s College. He went to Madras to study aerospace engineering from MIT. He was a great scientist and politician. He was the 11th President of India.
